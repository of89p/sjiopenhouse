This is where our story begins.

For the past 170 years, SJI has continuously empowered men and women to learn how to learn and to learn how to live.

Here at SJI, our stories can be more together.

A rite of passage, the orientation camp is where we formally become brothers and sisters to one another.

We are one, but we are many. Together, we hold the SJI banner high up with pride. A shield of green and white

Faith is our foundation.

At 7.10am daily, Catholics join together for morning prayer, while non-Catholic students remain in class. Once per week, Catholics are also invited for mass.

Service is our way. In all that we do, we serve the last, the lost and the least.

We understand, in this era of globalization, there is a lack of witness, a lack of example and a lack of action.

We believe that actions speak louder than words. Promoting action which empowers young people, we fully commit ourselves each day in the Lasallian Charism.

Community is our support.

And yet, above all the big school events we hold, it is the small things that mean the most to us

It is the simple friendships we are surrounded with. It is the simple good morning, the simple please, the simple thank you. It is the simple Discord movie nights some of us like to have, the simple study sessions we have together, the simple conversations we have with each other over lunch.

The small and simple, yet ever compelling things school brings for us.

The magic of SJI lies in the hope that, together, we can be better. Even during this pandemic, we hold strong to this hope.

Hope, not so much that life will be the same again, but hope that we can walk through our challenges together.

Hope, that we will make the most of these times. Hope that we will always remember these times, not for its empty promises, but for our growth through its difficulties.

This is the story we continue to write together.

“You can take a Josephian out of SJI, but you can never take SJI out of a Josephian.”

SJI is not just any place. It is our home. It is our family.
