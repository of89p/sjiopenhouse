Learning Minds

In SJI, we provide diverse programmes and opportunities for every Josephian to develop his or her talent or to pursue his passion. Through this, Josephians grow to become self-directed learners who are principled and reflective critical thinkers.



Learn More


Caring Hearts

In SJI, we believe in growing every student to become a Person of Integrity and a Person for Others - a Josephian who lives life such that the world becomes a better place because of him or her.



Learn More


Transforming Lives

In SJI, our academic and student development curricula focus on providing a balanced and holistic education with a strong emphasis on character formation and leadership development that will enable our students to live a meaningful life driven by passion in the service for others.
